The MISRA C 2004 compliance rule 14.8 applies to this code because it specifies that the statement forming the body of a switch statement must be a compound statement. The evidence used to detect this violation is the LLM (Language Model) inference, which identified the switch statement and determined that its body is not a compound statement.
